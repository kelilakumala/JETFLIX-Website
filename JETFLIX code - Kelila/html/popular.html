Top 5 Movies

#1

La La Land

When Sebastian, a pianist, and Mia, an actress, follow their passion and achieve success in their respective fields, they find themselves torn between their love for each other and their careers.

Genre: Musical, Romance, Comedy

Cast: Emma Stone, Ryan Reynolds, John Legend

#2

Friends

Friends is a 90's Comedy TV show, based in Manhattan, about 6 friends who go through just about every life experience imaginable together; love, marriage, divorce, children, heartbreaks, fights, new jobs and job losses and all sorts of drama.

Genre: Sitcoms, TV Comedy

Cast: Jennifer Anniston, Courtney Cox, Matthew Perry

#3

True Beauty

After being bullied and discriminated because of her ugly looks, a high school girl transforms herself into a gorgeous "goddess" and quickly rises to fame after mastering the art of makeup via online tutorial videos.

Genre: Romance, K-Drama

Cast: Moon Ga-young, Cha Eun-woo, Hwang In-Youp

#4

Bridgerton

Anthony Bridgerton navigates the intricate world of romance and societal expectations as he searches for a suitable wife. But when an enigmatic woman from his past resurfaces, it throws his plans into disarray, challenging his heart and family's reputation.

Genre: Romance, Drama

Cast: Adjoa Andoh, Julie Andrews, Jonathan Bailey

#5

Knives Out

Harlan Thrombey, a reputable crime novelist, is found dead after his 85th birthday celebrations. However, as detective Benoit Blanc investigates the case, it unravels a ploy of sinister intentions.

Genre: Crime, Drama

Cast: Daniel Craig, Chris Evans, Ana de Armas
